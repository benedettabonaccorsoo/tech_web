Documentation Help

Learning Agreement is the document which defines your study plan. You need to agree it with your sending university and wait for the hosting university acceptance of it. The outcome may depend on courses availabilities and other factors such as courses clash or ETCS balancing. You should have about the same total credits between your courses and hosting university ones. You have the chance to change the learning agreement, during your studies, in that case you will fill the During the mobility or after mobility learning agreement.

TOR (Transcript of Records) is the document you need in order to have credit conversion. It contains all the courses you attended and the possibile results

These documents certify your arrival at the university and your departure. Your sending institution may ask for them in order to provide any scolarship.
