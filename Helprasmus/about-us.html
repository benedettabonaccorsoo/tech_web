This is us

Our Story

Helprasmus was born out of the passion of two students who had the incredible opportunity to experience Erasmus and met during this extraordinary period. Erasmus is amazing, but students often face significant challenges with bureaucracy and the various steps required to realize the dream of living abroad, especially if it's their first time doing something like that. Unfortunately, many students give up on Erasmus, and that's a shame! We agreed that students need a tool to simplify the process and allow them to live the experience without being burdened by external and intrusive thoughts that could take away the fun and educational aspects. This is the aim of Helprasmus: to help students overcome obstacles and encourage them to embark on this amazing journey.

Our Commitment

We are two passionate students determined to ease the path for anyone looking to undertake the adventure of Erasmus. With Helprasmus, we aim to be the support that students seek to face bureaucratic challenges and make the departure process more accessible and enjoyable. Our mission is to encourage students not to give up on the Erasmus dream and to fully embrace this extraordinary opportunity.

Our Team

At the moment, there are only two of us managing Helprasmus:

  • Benedetta, is our expert in finding creative solutions to simplify the Erasmus process.
  • Matteo, handles practical management and communication with students seeking our help.
Together, we work tirelessly to ensure that Helprasmus is an effective and friendly tool for all students looking to embark on the Erasmus journey. We are here to guide you through every step of the process and make your Erasmus experience unforgettable.
